Step 3 — Visit fubo.tv/link6

On your phone or computer, go to fubo.tv/link6, sign in with your Fubo account, and enter the activation code.

Confirm linking and your device will refresh, giving you full access to Fubo TV content.

Troubleshooting common problems

Code expired

Activation codes are time-sensitive. Generate a new one by selecting Sign in again on your TV.

Connectivity issues

Check your Wi-Fi or wired connection. Restart your router and device if needed.

Wrong account

Ensure you are signing in with the same Fubo account linked to your subscription.

Outdated app

Update the Fubo TV app from your device’s app store for smooth activation.
